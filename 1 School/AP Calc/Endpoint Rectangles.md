$$
Right
$$
A\approx\sum_{i=1}^{n}\Delta{x}*f(a+\Delta{x}i)
$$
Middle
$$
A\approx\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}\Delta{x}*f(a+\frac{\Delta{x}}{2}+\Delta{x}i)
$$
Trapezoid Rule   ($\Delta{x}=h$)
$$
A_{trapezoid}=\frac{1}{2}(a+b)h
$$
